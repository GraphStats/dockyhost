Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our service.

How can I create an account?

Creating an account on DockyHost is simple and quick. Click the "Create an Account" button at the top of the page, choose a username and password, and that's it! You can immediately start creating your servers.

Are there really no hidden fees?

Absolutely! Our free plan is truly free for life, with no hidden fees. We earn through our premium users who choose to support our service and gain additional features.

What types of servers can I host?

You can host almost any type of server: games (Minecraft, FiveM, etc.), websites, applications, databases, Discord bots, and more. The only limitation is that your usage must comply with our terms of service.

How can I manage my servers?

We provide an intuitive control panel where you can start, stop, restart your servers, manage files, view real-time logs, and configure settings. You also have full SSH access for advanced control.

What are the limits of free servers?

Free servers can run 24/7 but with limited resources (1 GB RAM, 1 vCPU). If your server uses too many resources for an extended period, it may be temporarily limited to ensure fair distribution among all users.
